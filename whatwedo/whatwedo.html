What We Do

We are a faith-based organization committed to working with vulnerable children and victims of different circumstances under the name Nsansa Village Community Development Mission. We provide shelter, foodstuffs, and offer educational support to children.

By Promoting the following projects our mission will be accomplished (together).

TO RESCUE

Education

The children living in Nsansa are given schooling. The teacher assesses the level each child is at and teaches them accordingly. Nsansa has accomplished further schooling at Chongwe. Once children have been counselled and it is felt that they can progress further. There is opportunity for the children to go to Chongwe a village 2 hours away from Lusaka. This is a boarding Primary school developed by Nsansa.

There is further building taking place which we hope to develop into a Secondary School. Chongwe is in an ideal location, because they live in the school these children are more focused on their education therefore exam results are high.

    TO RESTORE

    Agriculture Project

    This is at Chongwe village, a way of providing food for the children and teachers at the school. It enables skills training in Agriculture. Villages of Chongwe get involved, this enables the feeding of the whole community.

    Volunteering Opportunities Agriculture skills needed.

    Health for All Project

    Nsansa provides a health outreach to Chongwe Community. A good opportunity for foreign nurses/doctors to gain experience within Africa and Student Nurses to gain skills in emergency nursing by volunteering short or long-term. Nsansa provides Medical Care for the Children on the streets. This is given by a Registered Nurse.

    Under 5's Project

    Nsansa works once a month in Chongwe assisting in the under fives clinic. Mothers and babies under five are provided with regular check-ups on child development and the supply of vaccinations. This promotes good health within the Chongwe population.

    TO PROTECT

    Counselling

    We journey with children who have experienced trauma on the streets. Through art, music and sport we want to build a healed community.
