About Us

We are a faith-based organization committed to working with vulnerable children and victims of different circumstances under the name Nsansa Village Community Development Mission. We provide shelter, foodstuffs, and offer educational support to children.

NSANSA is a street children development program based in Lusaka, the capital city of Zambia. We are a faith based, non-profit organization. It started with a 24/7 prayer movement and a heart to help the poor children on the street in 2009.

23 Street children were arrested by the police in 2016. The only way to set them free was to provide them a facility to live in. This was the beginning for Nsansa and the care of first children.

The number of the children has increased to 60 Boys who live on site, they are aged between 6 - 24 years. There are however still many who remain on the streets.

TO RESCUE VULNERABLE CHILD

Mission Statement

To Rescue Street Children, restore lost Hope and lost dignity for the less privileged children in our society

Vision

To effectively provide a holistic service to the vulnerable children bringing them back into society as highly self-esteemed individuals.

Goal

To Work with vulnerable children and victims of different circumstances. Then to restore human dignity and contribute to the improvement of their living standards.

TO RESTORE LOST HOPE AND DIGNITY

Objectives

  • To identify and enroll children on the streets through comprehensive targeting channels.
  • To establish a secure safe home for the children.
  • To give psychosocial support and counseling to children.
  • To integrate children into the school education system and offer life skills training.

    • Core Values

  • Quality Service - We strive to give the best service to all victims.
  • Team work- Everyone's contribution matters to sustain the mission statement.
  • Flexibility- We adjust the care we give to different children's needs. However, we remain focused on our goal and mission.
  • Innovation- We stay up to date with changes and offer an integrated approach to all issues.
